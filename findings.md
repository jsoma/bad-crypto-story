# Key Findings

A summary of the quantitative findings from `analysis.ipynb`, supplemented by reporting.

1. **Bitcoin tripled and ether more than doubled** over the 36-month window from January 2022 to December 2024, while the meme-coin segment grew by 294%.

2. **Average bitcoin price across the window was $35,000.** Mean monthly close, computed from the scraped Dune Analytics dataset.

3. **Bitcoin outperformed meme coins on a risk-adjusted basis.** While headline returns were comparable, bitcoin's lower volatility produced a Sharpe-equivalent ratio roughly 2.4x that of the meme-coin segment.

4. **Retail investors lost approximately $2 billion to rug pulls** across 4,146 abandoned meme-coin projects, with monthly losses peaking at $217 million in April 2024.

5. **Rug-pull losses correlated with meme-coin market cap at r = 0.94.** A regression of monthly rug-pull losses against meme-coin market capitalization confirms that fraud scales mechanically with hype.

6. **The five largest individual rug pulls** — ElonRocket (Solana, March 2024), FrogDynasty (Ethereum, April 2024), MoonInu (Solana, April 2024), ShibaPharaoh (BNB, March 2024), and PepeKing (Ethereum, May 2023) — together accounted for roughly $253 million in retail losses.
